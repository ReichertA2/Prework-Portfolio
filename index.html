Hello World!

Obtained my Bachelors degree in Chemistry from Texas A&M University. Career path started as a technician at a third party lab. Then became a chemist and after a few years was promoted as a supervisor. After having 5 years of experience, I decided to continue expanding my knowledge. This led to working for ExxonMobil. Within this company, I was lab technician then became a chemist within 5 months. After two years as a chemist, I decided to work for the quality department and adapted new skill sets such as SQC (Statistical Quality Analysis). In addition, I had the opportunity to become a Blending and Demurrage analyst. I have enjoyed the challenge that these roles brought and the skills that have been acquired through them.
